Automotive AI Algorithm Development Intern

Mask2Former Semantic Segmentation

Combined and re-annotated Mapillary and ADE20k datasets to fulfill requirements for quadruped robots and autonomous vehicles. Converted to COCO format and trained a custom Mask2Former model using NVIDIA TAO Toolkit. Integrated into ROS2 for real-time video inference on robotic and vehicle platforms.

Person Re-Identification (Re-ID)

Built a real-time pedestrian detection and re-ID pipeline using YOLO11n and OSNet. Employed cosine similarity for feature matching and exported models in .onnx and .engine formats. Achieved real-time inference at ~25 FPS for surveillance, robotics, and automotive use cases.

Sim2Real Quadruped Robot Terrain Traversal

Developed an elevation mapping workflow for quadruped robots by integrating Gazebo and point clouds from Intel RealSense. Designed a point cloud sampling/processing pipeline and integrated RL gait models in simulation. Employed VIO to resolve sensor interruptions and implemented forward kinematics to compute foot coordinates. Deployed on Unitree Go2 for Sim2Real validation.
