Automotive AI Algorithm Development Intern

Sep. 2024 - Nov. 2024, Feb. 2025 -

Mask2Former Semantic Segmentation

Combined and re-annotated Mapillary and ADE20k datasets to fulfill requirements for quadruped robots and autonomous vehicles. Converted to COCO format and trained a custom Mask2Former model using NVIDIA TAO Toolkit. Integrated into ROS2 for real-time video inference on robotic and vehicle platforms.

Person Re-Identification (Re-ID)

Built a real-time pedestrian detection and re-ID pipeline using YOLO11n and OSNet. Employed cosine similarity for feature matching and exported models in .onnx and .engine formats. Achieved real-time inference at ~25 FPS for surveillance, robotics, and automotive use cases.

Sim2Real Quadruped Robot Terrain Traversal

Developed an elevation mapping workflow for quadruped robots in simulated environments by integrating Gazebo and point clouds generated by Intel RealSense camera. Designed and automated a point cloud sampling and processing pipeline, successfully integrating the reinforcement learning gait models in simulation to enhance terrain adaptability of the quadruped robot. Employed Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO) instead of traditional IMU-based odometry to locate robot to resolve sensor interruptions during sport mode transitions. Implemented forward kinematics to compute foot spatial coordinates by integrating joint angle data. Deployed the developed algorithm on Unitree Go2 to complete the Sim2Real validation.
